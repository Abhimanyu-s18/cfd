## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect information you provide:
- Name, email address, phone number (optional)
- Date of birth
- Country, city, and timezone
- Trading experience information (optional)
- Avatar or profile picture (optional)

### 1.2 KYC/Verification Documents
- Government-issued ID
- Proof of address
- Photographs for verification
- Information from identity verification services

### 1.3 Automatic Information
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Operating system
- Usage data (pages visited, features used, time spent)
- Trading activity (virtual positions, PnL, order history)
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

### 1.4 Cookies
- Authentication cookies (required for login)
- Preference cookies (remember your settings)
- Analytics cookies (understand usage patterns)
- Marketing cookies (with your consent)

## 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

### 2.1 Primary Uses
- Account creation and management
- Platform functionality and features
- KYC verification and compliance
- Customer support and technical assistance
- Security and fraud prevention
- Dispute resolution
- Legal obligation compliance

### 2.2 Analytics & Improvement
- Improve platform performance and reliability
- Understand user behavior and preferences
- Develop new features and improvements
- Monitor system health and security
- Generate anonymized statistics

### 2.3 Communications
- Service updates and important notices
- Security alerts and warnings
- Customer support responses
- Marketing communications (with consent)
- Legal notices and changes to policies

## 3. DATA SHARING

### 3.1 We Share Data With:
- Service providers (hosting, email, analytics, support)
- Legal authorities (if required by law)
- Payment processors (if applicable)
- Data processors under data processing agreements

### 3.2 We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share trading activity publicly (unless anonymized)
- Use data for purposes not disclosed in this policy
- Share data with marketers without consent
- Share data across unrelated services

## 4. DATA SECURITY

### 4.1 Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest (database encryption)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits and testing
- Password hashing (bcrypt with salt)
- Two-factor authentication (optional)
- Security monitoring and intrusion detection

### 4.2 Data Retention
- Account data: Until account deletion request
- KYC documents: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Trading records: 7 years (regulatory requirement)
- Audit logs: 7 years (security and compliance)
- Email/communication history: Until deletion request
- Cookies: Until expiration or deletion

## 5. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR & Privacy Laws)

### 5.1 Right to Access
- Request copy of all your personal data
- Response provided within 30 days
- Format: Structured, commonly used format

### 5.2 Right to Correction
- Update inaccurate or incomplete data
- Request made via account settings or email
- Corrections made promptly

### 5.3 Right to Deletion
- Request account and data deletion
- Except data required by law (KYC, tax records)
- May take up to 30 days for processing

### 5.4 Right to Portability
- Receive data in machine-readable format
- Transfer data to another service
- Request via email to privacy@cfdtrading.com

### 5.5 Right to Object
- Object to automated decision-making
- Object to profiling
- Object to marketing communications

### 5.6 Right to Restrict
- Limit how we use data
- Data retained but not processed
- Subject to certain exceptions

## 6. COOKIES

### 6.1 Types of Cookies We Use
- **Essential:** Required for platform function (login, security)
- **Functional:** Remember your preferences and settings
- **Analytics:** Understand usage patterns and feature usage
- **Marketing:** Show relevant ads (with consent)

### 6.2 Cookie Consent
- Consent obtained on first visit
- You can manage preferences in account settings
- Disabling cookies may limit functionality
- Third-party cookies governed by their privacy policies

### 6.3 Cookie Duration
- Session cookies: Until browser closed
- Persistent cookies: Up to 12 months
- Tracking cookies: Based on provider policy
